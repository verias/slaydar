Slaydar

Moderation, blacklist management, appeals, and community safety tools for Discord communities.

What is Slaydar?

Slaydar is a Discord moderation platform designed to help trusted communities manage blacklist information, review appeals, monitor known bad actors, and share moderation intelligence across participating servers.

Slaydar is developed and maintained by Hexadec and Verias.
